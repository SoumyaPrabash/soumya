About

Shaping Digital Security

As an experienced professional with a background in Industrial and Building Automation Systems, I've embarked on a new journey in cybersecurity, driven by a passion for safeguarding digital assets. Armed with a Master's in Software Systems specializing in Security from BITS Pilani and hands-on experience in AI-based malware detection during my MTech dissertation, I bring a unique blend of technical prowess and strategic insight to the table. With a solid foundation in network security, penetration testing, and the coveted CEH certification, I'm poised to tackle evolving cyber threats head-on, leveraging my expertise to fortify digital infrastructures and ensure resilient defenses.

My professional journey spans over five enriching years in the dynamic realm of Industrial & Building Automation systems, where I honed a strong foundation in understanding and optimizing complex systems. Guided by a passion for technology, I navigated through challenges, contributing significantly to cross-functional projects and gaining expertise in Building Automation Systems and industrial analog instruments.

Motivated by an ever-evolving digital landscape and a desire to tackle new challenges, I decided to pivot my career towards cybersecurity. Armed with a Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification and a Master's degree in Software Systems specialized in Security from BITS Pilani, I delved into the world of cybersecurity with a specific focus on artificial intelligence.
